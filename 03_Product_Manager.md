## Official FDA Regulatory Pages Relevant to Health-Tech PM

Understanding these pathways is directly relevant to product management in medical devices and digital health:

| Topic | Official Page | What It Is |
|-------|---------------|------------|
| 510(k) | https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/510k-clearances/medical-device-safety-and-510k-clearance-process | Premarket notification for devices substantially equivalent to existing devices |
| PMA | https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/premarket-approval-pma/pma-review-process | Premarket approval for higher-risk devices |
| De Novo | https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/premarket-submissions-selecting-and-preparing-correct-submission/de-novo-classification-request | Pathway for novel devices without a predicate |
| SaMD | https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/digital-health-center-excellence/software-medical-device-samd | Software intended for medical purposes |
| QMSR | https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/postmarket-requirements-devices/quality-management-system-regulation-qmsr | Quality system regulation; effective February 2, 2026 |
| Digital Health Center of Excellence | https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/digital-health-center-excellence | FDA's hub for digital health, AI/ML, SaMD oversight |
