# JHU BME Career Guide (2026)

> For Johns Hopkins University Biomedical Engineering students and recent graduates
> Three directions: Junior Data Analyst, Associate Consultant, Associate Product Manager
> Official-source edition -- revalidated April 17, 2026

---

## Source Standards

- Johns Hopkins sources: `jhu.edu`, `bme.jhu.edu`, `careers.jhuapl.edu`
- U.S. government sources: `.gov` (OPM, BLS, FDA, NIH, CDC, CMS, USAJobs)
- Company sources: each company's own official careers page
- Third-party platforms: listed as tools with links to their own official sites
- No unsourced claims about salary, acceptance rates, or prestige rankings
